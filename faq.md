# Frequently Asked Questions

## Shipping & Delivery

### How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the continental US. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. Expedited overnight shipping is available for select items.

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 30 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 10-14 business days, depending on customs clearance and destination. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

### How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package on our website using your order number, or directly on the carrier's website (USPS, UPS, or FedEx).

### What if my package is lost or damaged?
If your package doesn't arrive within the expected timeframe or arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours at support@example.com with photos if applicable. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue and will either reship or refund your order.

## Returns & Refunds

### What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all items. Products must be unused, in original packaging, and in resalable condition. Some items like personalized products or intimate apparel are final sale and cannot be returned.

### How do I start a return?
To initiate a return, email support@example.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a prepaid return label within 24 hours. Pack the item securely and drop it off at any authorized shipping location.

### When will I receive my refund?
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned item. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Please note that it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the credit to appear on your statement.

### Can I exchange an item?
Yes! If you'd like to exchange for a different size, color, or product, please indicate this when requesting your return. We'll prioritize shipping your exchange item as soon as we receive your return.

## Products

### Are your products eco-friendly?
Absolutely! We're committed to sustainability. We use organic materials whenever possible, eco-friendly packaging made from recycled materials, and partner with manufacturers who follow ethical and sustainable practices.

### Do you offer gift wrapping?
Yes! Gift wrapping is available for $5 per item at checkout. Your gift will be wrapped in premium paper with a ribbon and can include a personalized message card.

### Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?
Many of our products are hypoallergenic and dermatologist-tested. Product pages indicate if an item is suitable for sensitive skin. If you have specific allergies, please check the full ingredient list on the product page or contact us before purchasing.

### Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?
Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for orders of 50+ units. Please contact our wholesale team at wholesale@example.com with details about your business and the products you're interested in.

## Payment & Security

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. For orders over $500, we also accept bank transfers.

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways certified as PCI DSS compliant.

### Do you offer payment plans?
For orders over $100, we offer payment plans through Klarna and Afterpay. You can split your purchase into 4 interest-free payments. Simply select one of these options at checkout.

## Account & Orders

### Do I need an account to place an order?
No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save shipping addresses, view order history, and receive exclusive member discounts.

### How do I cancel or modify my order?
Orders can be cancelled or modified within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order goes into processing and cannot be changed. Contact us immediately at support@example.com if you need to make changes.

### Can I use multiple discount codes?
Only one discount code can be applied per order. If you have multiple codes, we recommend using the one that provides the greatest discount.
